Rules

Please adhere to the following rules to ensure an enjoyable experience for everyone.

1. Respect all players and staff members at all times.
2. No metagaming or powergaming is allowed.
3. Cheating, exploiting, or using unauthorized mods is strictly prohibited.
4. Roleplay realistically; do not perform actions your character cannot do in real life.
5. Keep all in-character (IC) conversations and actions in character.
6. Out-of-character (OOC) chat is for emergencies or quick clarifications only.
7. Do not grief or intentionally disrupt others' roleplay.
8. Player vs. player combat must have a valid roleplay reason.
9. Do not misuse or spam the report system.
10. Follow all in-game laws as if they were real-world laws.
11. No offensive or discriminatory language is allowed.
12. Keep the game environment safe for all players.
13. Report any rule violations to the staff immediately.
14. Do not use alternate accounts to bypass bans or gain an advantage.
15. Ensure your character's background aligns with the server's lore.
16. Avoid engaging in unrealistic or overly dramatic scenarios.
17. Respect the decisions of staff members; their word is final.
18. Do not impersonate staff members or other players.
19. Avoid excessive swearing or inappropriate content in voice or text chat.
20. All vehicle rules and traffic laws must be obeyed during roleplay.
21. No spamming or advertising other servers in any form.
22. Characters must have realistic names and backstories.
23. Do not engage in outlandish or superhero-like behavior.
24. Avoid AFK in public areas for extended periods.
25. Your actions must reflect the character's personality and history.
26. Avoid unrealistic use of weapons or vehicles.
27. Follow server-specific rules for each job or faction.
28. Do not abuse in-game features to gain an unfair advantage.
29. Avoid unnecessary OOC comments during roleplay scenarios.
30. Engage in meaningful and story-driven roleplay.
